# Add-ons

Anki's capabilities can be extended with add-ons. These extensions offer features including language-specific support and scheduling customization.

## Accessing Add-ons

To browse available add-ons, navigate to **Tools > Add-ons** and select **Get Add-ons**. Alternatively, visit [ankiweb.net/shared/addons](https://ankiweb.net/shared/addons) directly.

## Managing Add-ons

The **Delete** option in the menu removes problematic or unwanted add-ons from your installation.

## Compatibility Considerations

Updating Anki can break the compatibility with older add-ons. If an add-on ceases functioning after an Anki update, contact the add-on author to request an update. Users who depend on non-updated add-ons may need to maintain an older Anki version.

## Contacting Add-on Authors

Most add-ons pages on AnkiWeb feature a **Contact Author** button. Authors frequently include their email address within the add-on code itself — check the file's beginning for contact information.

## Learning to Create Add-ons

For guidance on developing custom add-ons, consult the [add-on writing guide](https://addon-docs.ankiweb.net).
